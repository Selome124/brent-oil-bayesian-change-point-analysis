# Brent Oil Price Change Point & Volatility Analysis

## Business Problem
Oil prices are highly sensitive to geopolitical events, policy decisions, and economic shocks. Investors, policymakers, and energy companies require reliable tools to detect structural market shifts and understand associated risks.

## Solution Overview
This project applies statistical and AI-based change point detection techniques to historical Brent oil prices and compares them with major geopolitical events. A volatility analysis and interactive dashboard enable transparent exploration of market regimes.

## Key Results
- Identified multiple statistically significant structural breaks in Brent oil prices
- Strong temporal alignment between geopolitical events and price regime shifts
- Interactive dashboard enabling non-technical stakeholders to explore impacts
